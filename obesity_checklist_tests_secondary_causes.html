Obesity Evaluation: Secondary Causes & Recommended Tests Checklist

Evidence-based checklist from AACE 2025, EASO 2024, KSSO 2022, Endocrine Society & OMA Guidelines

SECONDARY CAUSES OF OBESITY (Check if suspected)
Endocrine Disorders
Hypothyroidism (TSH)
Cushing’s syndrome (Cortisol, dexamethasone suppression)
Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS)
Hypogonadism (Testosterone, LH/FSH)
Growth hormone deficiency
Genetic / Syndromic Causes
Prader-Willi syndrome
Bardet-Biedl syndrome
Alström syndrome
Monogenic obesity (MC4R, LEPR mutations)
Medication-Induced
Glucocorticoids / Steroids
Antipsychotics (Olanzapine, Quetiapine, etc.)
Antidepressants (Mirtazapine, Paroxetine)
Antiepileptics (Valproate, Carbamazepine)
Insulin & Sulfonylureas
Beta-blockers, Hormonal contraceptives
Other Secondary Causes
Hypothalamic lesions / tumors / injury
Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA)
Depression / Psychiatric disorders
Iatrogenic (Post-bariatric surgery weight regain)
RECOMMENDED TESTS & SCREENINGS
Anthropometric Assessment
Height, Weight, BMI calculation
Waist circumference (men >102 cm, women >88 cm)
Waist-to-height ratio (>0.5)
Body composition (BIA / DXA) – if available
Basic Laboratory Tests
Fasting plasma glucose / HbA1c
Lipid profile (Total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, TG)
Liver function tests (ALT, AST, GGT)
TSH (Thyroid function)
Complete blood count (CBC)
Renal function (Creatinine, eGFR)
Uric acid
Additional / Targeted Tests
75g OGTT (if fasting glucose inconclusive)
STOP-BANG or polysomnography (for OSA)
ECG (if cardiac risk)
Testosterone / LH / FSH (men with hypogonadism symptoms)
Cortisol testing (if Cushing’s suspected)
Overnight Dexamethasone Suppression Test (ONDST)
Pelvic ultrasound + hormonal profile (PCOS)
USG Whole Abdomen (NAFLD, gallstones, PCOS)
Fibroscan (liver fibrosis assessment)
Fundus examination (diabetic retinopathy screening)
Full thyroid profile: T3, T4 + TSH
Serum Amylase & Lipase
Genetic testing (early onset severe obesity)
Comorbidity Screening
Blood pressure (every visit)
Depression screening (PHQ-9)
Eating disorder screening
Cancer screening (age-appropriate)
