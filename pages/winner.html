📋 Patient Profile & Program Philosophy

Program Philosophy: Obesity is a chronic, manageable condition. This 36-month journey is designed not just for rapid weight loss, but for metabolic reset, lean muscle preservation, and lifelong sustainable habits using the triad of GLP-1 therapy, optimized nutrition, and targeted exercise.

🛡️ The 3 Pillars of Success — Patient Agreement

1. 💊 Medical — GLP-1 Adherence

  • I understand GLP-1 medications reduce appetite and delay gastric emptying.
  • I will communicate any persistent nausea, reflux, or GI distress to my provider before adjusting my dose.
  • I understand this medication is a tool, not a cure, and works synergistically with my lifestyle choices.

2. 🥗 Nutritional — Dietary Strategy

  • Protein First: I will prioritize protein at every meal to protect my muscle mass while losing weight.
  • Hydration & Fiber: I will consume adequate water and fiber to support digestion and prevent medication-induced constipation.
  • Nutrient Density: Since my overall food volume will decrease, I will focus on high-quality, nutrient-dense whole foods.

3. 🏋️ Physical — Exercise Protocol

  • Resistance Training: I commit to lifting weights or doing bodyweight resistance at least 2 days/week to signal my body to keep muscle and burn fat.
  • NEAT (Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis): I will aim to stay generally active throughout the day beyond my structured workouts.
