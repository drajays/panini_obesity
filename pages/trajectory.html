Part 5: Clinical Reframe & Core First Principles

1. Removes Patient Stigma

Plotting the biological weight curve from birth visually proves to the patient that obesity is a chronic, multi-year, progressive biological adaptation—not a sudden personal failure or a lack of willpower.

Reframes guilt into biological objectivity

2. Manages Expectations & Plateaus

Visually demonstrating the mathematical "flattening" of the curve between Month 12 and Month 18 prevents patient panic when rapid weight loss inevitably slows down. It normalizes the plateau as a clinical success.

Eliminates yo-yo abandonment risk

3. Reframes Long-Term Success

If a patient regains 2 kg in Year 3 or encounters a minor stress event, they can examine their lifetime trajectory map and verify that they fundamentally remain completely detached from their historical steep upward velocity.

Anchors permanent behavior adaptation

Clinical Architecture Guide & Workflow
1

Complete Patient Demographics (Page 1)

Enter the patient's baseline weight, lifetime peak weight, height, and age. The system instantly evaluates their current Baseline BMI, Peak BMI, and establishes obesity classification boundaries.

2

Populate Historical 10-kg Milestones (Page 1)

Click "Reset 10-kg Increments" or add specific historical weight nodes. Ask the patient to recall approximately what age they reached 20kg, 40kg, 60kg, 80kg, 100kg, etc., and document major associated life events (e.g., college, marriage, pregnancy, high-stress occupations).

3

Log past weight loss attempts (Page 1)

Click + Add for each program (Keto, Weight Watchers, etc.). Enter age, kg lost, how long they kept it, why it worked, and why weight returned.

4

Model the 3-Year Prognostic Map & Present Chart (Page 2)

Select a modern therapy pathway (e.g., GLP-1 Intensive -20% TBWL) or set a custom target. The clinical calculator constructs exact physiological expectations across Month 0, Month 6, Month 12, Month 18, and Year 3 Maintenance. The Master Visual Graph renders in real time.

5

Print or Save 2-Page Patient Handout

Click "Print 2-Page Report" to instantly generate an immaculate, professional 2-page physical or PDF clinical summary optimized specifically for the patient's physical chart or take-home instructional booklet.
