Psychological & Behavioral Readiness Assessment

For patients starting chronic weight management with nutrition, activity and GLP‑1/GIP or GLP‑1 therapy. Chronic weight management relies heavily on behavioral adaptation. Identifying untreated psychological barriers early is critical. Screening tool only — not a diagnosis.
How to fill: Tap a number (0–3) in each PHQ-9 / GAD-7 row · check Yes boxes · type in text fields. Everything saves automatically.
